Things your bunny will do when they're happy:

  • Binkies - Bunnies can start running around, jumping and kicking when they are extremely happy.
  • Flopping - Sometimes a relaxed, content and safe bunny will suddenly fall over.
  • Grind teeth softly - When being pet, bunnies will start grinding thier teeth. This is their way of purring.
